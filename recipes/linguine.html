Garlic-Herb Linguine

Description

A super simple pasta dish for super simple pasta people! This is easy comfort food at it's finest and easiest. You just need some noodles and other odds and ends, then you're on your way to flavor town. Unfortunately, odds and ends aren't included in this economy, but if you happen to have some herbs and such on hand, this dish is a breeze. This pasta keeps it simple, but has subtle depths of flavor that will induce visions of Italy from the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces dried linguine
  • Â¼ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook linguine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 10 to 11 minutes. Drain and transfer to a large bowl.

  2. Mix melted butter, garlic, and parsley together in a small bowl. Drizzle butter mixture over linguine. Add seasoned salt and toss to coat.
