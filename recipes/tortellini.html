Creamy Sausage Tortellini Soup

Description

This is a savory and delicious creamy sausage tortellini soup! If you use spicy sausage it's perfect for a cold Winter day. The vegetables are simple, but add a pop of red and green that stand out in the orange creamy base and elevate the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound hot Italian sausage
  • 3/4 cups sliced celery
  • 3/4 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 14 1/2 ounces chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can basil, garlic, and oregano diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 (8.8 ounce) package cheese tortellini
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place sausage, celery, carrots and onions in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Cook and stir until sausage is browned and veggies are tender, 7 to 9 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  2. Stir in flour and until well incorporated. Pour in chicken broth, scraping bottom of pot to release all browned bits. Cook for 1 minute. Add diced tomatoes and Italian seasoning; bring to a boil.

  3. Reduce heat, add tortellini, and simmer for 5 minutes. Add cream; cook and stir for 5 more minutes. Add mozzarella cheese and stir until melted; stir in spinach. Season with salt and pepper.
