Guacamole

Description

This guacamole is a spicy green dish that'll send your taste buds flying into next week. It's got a smooth or chunky texture depending on how much you want to mash it up! Add in some citrus and jalapenos and you'll be crying for more. Serve with some salty tortilla chips to up the ante and offer that classic crunch. Perfect for a late night snack or lunch at the office.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.


  2. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.


  3. Stir in cayenne pepper. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
